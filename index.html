SILICONE COATINGS

Our silicone coatings used on the outer surfaces of cookware, ovenware and small household appliances offer a wide range of color options and can be applied in different gloss levels, whether matte or glossy, smooth or with metallic effect. Silicone coatings are highly heat- resistant and easy to clean.

  • They offer a wide range of color options
  • They offer design richness with different spatter sizes and densities
  • White or gold spider effect is applicable to all silicone coating options
  • A second coat can be applied to give a silvery appearance
  • Reapplication is possible on the cured coating

PTFE COATINGS

Fluoropolymer coatings, with their unique molecular structure of a long carbon chain saturated with fluorine atoms and strong inter-atomic bonds, exhibit high inertness. They offer wear and chemical resistance, low friction, and do not adhere to surfaces. Widely used across various industries, fluoropolymers stand out for their superior temperature resistance and broader operating temperature range compared to other polymers.

  • Fluoropolymer-based coatings have highly inert properties
  • They exhibit wear resistance, chemical resistance, and low friction
  • Fluoropolymers do not stick to any substance
  • Widely used in various sectors due to higher temperature resistance and a broader operating temperature range compared to other polymers
